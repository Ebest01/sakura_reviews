Our Commitment to Authentic Reviews

At Sakura Reviews, we believe that authentic reviews are the foundation of trust between merchants and customers. We are committed to maintaining the integrity of reviews displayed through our platform.

üõ°Ô∏è Our Promise: We actively work to prevent fake, fraudulent, and misleading reviews from appearing on merchant stores.

What We Consider Review Fraud

The following activities are strictly prohibited:

  • Fake Reviews: Reviews created by the merchant, their employees, or paid services that pretend to be from genuine customers
  • Incentivized Reviews: Offering payment, discounts, or free products in exchange for positive reviews without disclosure
  • Review Manipulation: Selectively importing only positive reviews while hiding negative ones
  • False Attribution: Attributing reviews to products they were not written for
  • Competitor Sabotage: Posting fake negative reviews on competitor products
  • Review Spam: Posting duplicate or irrelevant reviews

Merchant Responsibilities

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Merchants using Sakura Reviews must comply with all applicable laws regarding consumer reviews, including:
  • FTC Endorsement Guidelines (USA)
  • Consumer Protection from Unfair Trading Regulations (UK)
  • EU Omnibus Directive
  • Local consumer protection laws

Merchants agree to:

  • Only display authentic reviews from real customers
  • Not manipulate or falsify review content
  • Disclose any material connections with reviewers
  • Not suppress negative reviews unfairly
  • Respond to reviews honestly and professionally

Reporting Fraudulent Reviews

If you suspect a review is fake or fraudulent, please report it to us:

  • Email: sakura.revs@gmail.com
  • Include the store URL and review details
  • We will investigate within 5 business days

Consequences of Violations

Merchants found to be engaging in review fraud may face:

  • Removal of fraudulent reviews
  • Suspension of import privileges
  • Account termination without refund
  • Reporting to Shopify Trust & Safety
  • Legal action in serious cases

Transparency Settings

To comply with the EU Omnibus Directive and promote transparency, merchants can:

  • Display "Verified Purchase" indicators
  • Show review dates and reviewer locations
  • Enable "Most Recent" sorting for customers
  • Display both positive and negative reviews
