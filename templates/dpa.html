Data Processing Addendum

Last Updated: December 2025

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") forms part of the Terms of Service between Sakura Reviews ("Processor", "we", "us") and you ("Controller", "Merchant") and governs the processing of personal data.

1. Definitions

"Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person as defined by applicable Data Protection Laws.
"Data Protection Laws" means GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and other applicable privacy regulations.
"Processing" means any operation performed on Personal Data, including collection, storage, use, and deletion.
"Sub-processor" means any third party engaged by us to process Personal Data on your behalf.

2. Roles and Responsibilities

You (Merchant) are the Data Controller: You determine the purposes and means of processing your customers' Personal Data.

Sakura Reviews is the Data Processor: We process Personal Data on your behalf according to your instructions.

3. Scope of Processing

Categories of Data Subjects

  • Your store customers who submit reviews
  • Individuals whose reviews are imported from other platforms

Types of Personal Data

Data Type Processing Purpose
Name/Display name Review attribution
Review content Display on product pages
Star ratings Aggregate rating calculation
Photos/Videos Visual review display
Location/Country Review context (optional)
Purchase verification Verified buyer badges

4. Our Obligations as Processor

We agree to:

  • Process Personal Data only on your documented instructions
  • Ensure personnel are bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
  • Assist you in responding to data subject requests
  • Delete or return Personal Data upon termination (as requested)
  • Make available information necessary to demonstrate compliance
  • Notify you of any Personal Data breach without undue delay

5. Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

  • Encryption: TLS/SSL for data in transit, encryption at rest
  • Access Control: Role-based access, authentication requirements
  • Monitoring: Security logging and anomaly detection
  • Infrastructure: Secure hosting with regular backups
  • Personnel: Security training and confidentiality agreements

6. Sub-processors

You authorize us to engage the following sub-processors:

Sub-processor Purpose Location
Cloud Hosting Provider Infrastructure and storage EU/US
Shopify App platform and billing Canada/US
Database Provider Data storage EU

We will notify you before adding or replacing sub-processors, giving you the opportunity to object.

7. International Data Transfers

When transferring Personal Data outside the EEA/UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards including:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • UK International Data Transfer Agreement
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable

8. Data Subject Rights

We will assist you in fulfilling data subject requests including:

  • Access requests
  • Rectification requests
  • Erasure requests ("right to be forgotten")
  • Data portability requests
  • Objection to processing

9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a Personal Data breach, we will:

  • Notify you within 72 hours of becoming aware
  • Provide details of the breach nature and scope
  • Describe measures taken to mitigate impact
  • Cooperate in any required notifications to authorities or data subjects

10. Audit Rights

Upon reasonable notice, you may:

  • Request information about our security practices
  • Review audit reports and certifications
  • Conduct audits (subject to confidentiality and reasonable scope)

11. Term and Termination

This DPA remains in effect for the duration of the Service agreement. Upon termination:

  • We will delete your Personal Data within 90 days
  • Or return it to you upon request
  • Except where retention is required by law

12. Governing Law

This DPA is governed by the same laws as the main Terms of Service, with specific provisions of Data Protection Laws taking precedence where applicable.
