Importing Reviews from AliExpress

Easily import reviews from any AliExpress product page into your Shopify store

Overview

With Sakura Reviews, you can import authentic customer reviews from AliExpress products and display them on your Shopify store. This is perfect for dropshippers and merchants who source products from AliExpress.

‚ú® Why Import from AliExpress?
  • Build social proof before you have your own reviews
  • Show real customer experiences from the source
  • Import photos and ratings automatically
  • AI-powered quality scoring ensures only the best reviews

Prerequisites

Before you start, make sure you have:

  • ‚úÖ Sakura Reviews app installed on your Shopify store
  • ‚úÖ A Shopify product created for the item you want to import reviews to
  • ‚úÖ The AliExpress product URL for the item
  • ‚úÖ Your Shopify product ID (found in your product URL or admin panel)

Step-by-Step Guide

1 Find Your AliExpress Product

Go to AliExpress and find the product you want to import reviews from. Copy the product URL from your browser's address bar.

Example: https://www.aliexpress.com/item/1005004632823451.html
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Make sure the AliExpress product matches your Shopify product. Reviews should be relevant to what you're actually selling.

2 Get Your Shopify Product ID

You'll need your Shopify product ID to link the reviews. Here's how to find it:

  1. Go to your Shopify admin panel
  2. Navigate to Products
  3. Click on the product you want to import reviews to
  4. Look at the URL - it will look like: https://admin.shopify.com/store/your-store/products/1234567890
  5. The number at the end (e.g., 1234567890) is your Product ID
Product ID Example: 10045740417338

3 Access the Import Tool

There are two ways to import reviews:

Method A: Via App Dashboard (Recommended)

  1. Open Sakura Reviews in your Shopify admin
  2. Click on "Import Reviews" in the dashboard
  3. Select "AliExpress" as the platform
  4. Paste your AliExpress product URL
  5. Enter your Shopify product ID
  6. Click "Preview Reviews"

Method B: Via API Endpoint (Advanced)

You can also use the import API directly:

GET /admin/reviews/import/url?productId=ALIEXPRESS_PRODUCT_ID&platform=aliexpress&url=ALIEXPRESS_URL

4 Preview and Select Reviews

After entering the URL, you'll see a preview of available reviews. You can:

  • Filter by rating: Show only 4-5 star reviews
  • Filter by photos: Show only reviews with images
  • Filter by country: Show reviews from specific countries
  • Select individual reviews: Choose which reviews to import
  • Import all: Import all available reviews at once
üí° Pro Tip: Our AI quality scoring automatically highlights the best reviews. Look for reviews with high quality scores (8.0+) for maximum impact!

5 Import Reviews

Once you've selected the reviews you want:

  1. Click "Import Selected Reviews"
  2. Wait for the import to complete (usually takes a few seconds)
  3. You'll see a success message with the number of reviews imported
‚úÖ What Happens During Import:
  • Reviews are saved to your database
  • Photos are downloaded and stored
  • AI quality scores are calculated
  • Reviews are linked to your Shopify product
  • Reviews appear automatically on your product page

6 Verify Reviews Are Displaying

After importing, check your product page:

  1. Go to your Shopify storefront
  2. Navigate to the product page
  3. Scroll down to see the reviews widget
  4. You should see the imported reviews with photos, ratings, and customer names

What Gets Imported

For each review, we import:

Data Description
Reviewer Name Customer's name (may be partially anonymized)
Rating Star rating (1-5 stars)
Review Text Full review content
Photos Customer-uploaded product photos
Review Date When the review was posted
Country Reviewer's country (if available)
Verified Badge Indicates if the reviewer actually purchased the product

AI Quality Scoring

Sakura Reviews uses advanced AI to score each review's quality (0-10 scale). Reviews are scored based on:

  • Content Quality: Length, detail, helpfulness
  • Photo Quality: Number and clarity of photos
  • Authenticity: Language patterns and credibility
  • Engagement: Helpful votes and interactions
‚≠ê AI Recommended: Reviews with a quality score of 8.0 or higher are marked as "AI Recommended" and can be featured prominently on your product pages.

Best Practices

  • Match Products Correctly: Only import reviews for products that match what you're selling
  • Import Recent Reviews: Focus on reviews from the last 6-12 months for relevance
  • Mix Ratings: Don't only import 5-star reviews - a mix looks more authentic
  • Check Photos: Reviews with photos are more valuable - prioritize those
  • Respect Limits: Be aware of your plan's review limits (Free: 50, Basic: 500, Pro: 5,000)

Troubleshooting

‚ùå "No reviews found"

Possible causes:

  • The AliExpress product has no reviews
  • The product URL is incorrect
  • The product ID couldn't be extracted from the URL

Solution: Double-check the AliExpress URL and make sure the product has reviews.

‚ùå "Import failed" or "Error connecting"

Possible causes:

  • Network connectivity issues
  • AliExpress blocking requests (temporary)
  • Invalid product ID format

Solution: Wait a few minutes and try again. If the problem persists, contact support.

‚ùå "Reviews imported but not showing on product page"

Possible causes:

  • Shopify product ID doesn't match
  • Widget not installed on product template
  • Reviews are pending approval

Solution:

  1. Verify the Shopify product ID is correct
  2. Check that the widget ScriptTag is installed
  3. Go to Reviews ‚Üí Manage and ensure reviews are published

‚ùå "Reached review limit"

Solution: Upgrade your plan to import more reviews, or delete old reviews to free up space.

Limitations & Considerations

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes:
  • Review Authenticity: Only import reviews for products you actually sell. Importing reviews for unrelated products violates our Review Integrity Policy.
  • Legal Compliance: Ensure you have the right to use and display these reviews. See our Review Integrity Policy.
  • Rate Limits: To prevent abuse, there may be rate limits on how many reviews you can import per day.
  • Photo Storage: Photos are stored on our servers. Large imports may take time to process.
