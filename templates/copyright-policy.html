Sakura Reviews ("we", "us", "our") respects the intellectual property rights of others and requests that our users do the same.

Alleged Infringement Notice (DMCA)

If you believe that content available through our Service infringes your copyrights, you may send our designated copyright agent a written notification that includes:

  1. A physical or electronic signature of the person authorized to act on behalf of the owner of the right that is allegedly infringed;
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed;
  3. Identification of the content that is claimed to infringe, with information sufficient to permit us to locate the content (include URLs where possible);
  4. Information sufficient to permit us to contact you, such as an address, telephone number, and email address;
  5. A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law;
  6. A statement that the information in the notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner of the copyrighted work.

Counter Notification

If we've removed or disabled access to content you submitted pursuant to a claimed infringement notification, you may submit a counter-notification that includes:

  1. Your physical or electronic signature;
  2. Identification of the removed content and the location where it appeared before removal;
  3. A statement under penalty of perjury that you believe the content was removed as a result of mistake or misidentification;
  4. Your name, address, and telephone number, and a statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of applicable courts and will accept service of process.

After receiving a valid counter-notification, we may restore the content within 10-14 business days, unless the original complainant files a court action.

Repeat Infringers

We may terminate user accounts of repeat infringers. A repeat infringer is a user who has been notified of infringing activity more than twice or whose content was removed more than twice.

Review Content and Photos

Important: Merchants are responsible for ensuring they have the right to use and display review content, including photos and videos, that they import from external platforms.

When importing reviews from third-party platforms, merchants should:

  1. Only import reviews for products they actually sell
  2. Respect the original reviewer's rights
  3. Not modify review content in a misleading way
  4. Remove content promptly if requested by the original author

Misrepresentations

Any person who knowingly misrepresents that content is infringing, or that content was removed by mistake, may be liable for damages, including costs and attorneys' fees.
